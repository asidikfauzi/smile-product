Seamless Mobile Identification Location Equipment.

SMILE is a tactical equipment consisting of a device attached to a specially modified vehicle and also a portable device that can be included in a bag in general, the SMILE tactical device can collaborate in identifying the location of the mobile device used by the target, working silently (undetected) and efficient in conducting intelligence operations.

Feature

All in One

Supports all networks:
GSM
WCDMA
TDD-LTE
FDD-LTE
5G-NSA

Backend System Supports

  • Get target Cell-ID information with MSISDN parameter.
  • Get the target Cell-ID coordinates.
  • Sending Silent SMS to the target, without the target being aware of it.

Data Synchronization

  • To avoid blind spots, there are two ways to synchronize data on devices, namely by radio waves and internet data networks.
  • After synchronization, as long as one device can get energy and other devices don't need to parse the base station signal, it can automatically get energy.

High Accuracy

High degree of accuracy, less than 1 meter.

Totally Passive

  • Does not carry out radio transmission (radiation), does not interfere with the operator's network.
  • Does not cause radiation which is harmful to the user.
  • Well camouflaged.
  • Does not raise target perception.

Team Working

  • The main device in the vehicle can collaborate with several portable devices.
  • Each device can see each other's status and work together.
  • Information on each device is automatically synchronized.
